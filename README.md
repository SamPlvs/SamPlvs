# Sam Tukra.

> I design &amp; build **AI models**.
> Chief AI Officer &amp; Co-Founder at **[Applied Computing](https://samtukra.com/)** — turning frontier ML research into systems that run in live industrial environments.

I've spent the last 10 years building intelligent systems — leading full-stack ML teams from R&amp;D to production across academia, the energy industry, and startups. Today I work on **Orbital**, a physics-grounded multi-agent co-pilot that optimises large industrial process operations, starting with oil &amp; gas refineries.

My work bridges deep learning, physics-based modelling, multi-agent systems &amp; reinforcement learning to deliver AI that's not just smart, but **operational as a product**.
